WHO WE ARE

A New Standard of Air Travel in Nigeria

Bizijet Air is a fully indigenous Nigerian airline committed to delivering safe, affordable and reliable passenger, cargo and mail transportation services across Nigeria and regional African destinations.

We are building more than an airline—we are creating a customer-focused aviation brand driven by operational excellence, innovation and world-class passenger care.
