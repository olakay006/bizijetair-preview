WHO WE ARE

A New Generation Nigerian Airline

Bizijet Air is a fully indigenous Nigerian airline being established to provide scheduled and unscheduled passenger, cargo and mail transport services while delivering a safe, reliable and cost-efficient travel experience across Nigeria and beyond.

As a hybrid airline, Bizijet combines affordable fares with premium service standards, strategic planning, operational excellence and a strong commitment to passenger care.
